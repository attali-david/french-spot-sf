A MODERN PASTRY CONCEPT

The French Spot brings French culture with a modern twist to your door with our San Francisco Bay Area subscription service. Or choose from an ever-changing menu of French-Asian-American pastries, desserts, breads, and chocolates crafted from local ingredients available at our Saturday popup! San Franciscans now have a convenient way to reward themselves without the risk.

Working out of Belmont in a commercial kitchen, our goal is to continue bringing you amazing quality pastries. Plus the convenience of delivery, with optional pickups! Our focus is on wholesale, subscription pastry packages and regular weekend popups. We also can create any type of custom cake, the bigger the challenge the better! Teambuilding corporate classes or family pastry lessons are available as well.

Meet Maria and Vincent

Maria is an art nerd, with a love of oil paints, sculpting & Adobe Illustrator. Pastry credentials include working with Chocolatier Jean Marie Auboine in Las Vegas, pastry sous chef in the East Bay and custom cake work..

Vincent is a former graphic designer and 2nd generation pastry chef, he has been working over 15 years in 3 Michelin star restaurants (Robuchon Las Vegas, Daniel), high end pastry shops (Fauchon, Payard, Financier), and luxury hotels (Wynn Las Vegas).

Recently the Exec Pastry Chef at LinkedIn, Vincent has won multiple national competitions. He's also appeared on tv, international newspapers & magazines. Consultant on restaurant openings, bakery production modifications, artisanal confections and menu creation. Basically, he loves all things pastry related.
